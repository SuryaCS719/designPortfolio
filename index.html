Visual Design Examples

Cross-Cultural Design Interpretation

This project demonstrates the understanding of how visual elements are interpreted across different cultures. It explores how cultural context influences design choices and user experience.

Chinese Wedding: Red symbolizes good fortune and joy.
Western Business: Blue represents trust, stability, and professionalism.
Irish Culture: Green is associated with St. Patrick's Day and nature.
Indian Festival: Yellow represents celebration and energy.
Thai Culture: Purple is the color of mourning.
Western Fashion: Black symbolizes elegance and sophistication.

Design Principles: Cultural Sensitivity, Visual Semiotics
